Welcome to Our Language Register Platform

Our goal is to provide language courses for refugees to help them integrate, communicate and build a better future. Our courses are designed to suit a variety of skill levels, from beginner to advanced, and are tailored to the unique needs of our students.


Available Courses

  • Basic English Course
  • Conversational English
  • Advanced English
  • Cultural Orientation
  • Business English
  • Grammar Intensive Course
  • Writing Skills Workshop
  • Pronunciation Practice

Important information

Please note that registration for any of our courses requires the purchase of specific textbooks and materials, which are necessary to fully participate and succeed in the program. By enrolling, you commit to attending all classes and completing assignments. We strive to make these resources as affordable as possible and are here to support you every step of the way.
